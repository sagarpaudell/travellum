How it works?

Travellum provides a platform to connect travelers. It helps you find the perfect person to travel with. Maybe you want a guide who knows everything about the history of the place or maybe you are just searching for a companion to travel with. We’ve got you covered. We have a ton of travel guides you can choose from based on their rating. But instead, if you are thinking of employing yourself as a travel guide, you can do that from travellum. It is as easy as opening a facebook account.

We are trying our best to introduce freelancing to the travel industry. If you really think you are capable of being a guide, we don’t ask you for your travel degree, you can simply register to our site. We will help you find the travellers who are looking for guides exactly like you. You can earn a decent amount of money without sacrificing your time in 9-5 job. Work whenever you want and earn the amount you deserve.

We know traveling in a new place can be scary and as a human we need someone to accompany us when we are going someplace new. We can help you find the perfect person who can be your best bud while traveling. We know that you love meeting new people and going to new places. So why not choose who you want to travel with? Who knows, your perfect travel buddy could be the one you’ve been searching your whole life for.
